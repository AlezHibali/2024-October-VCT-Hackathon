  • ChiaWei is selected as the IGL due to his experience playing both initiator and duelist roles. This versatility allows him to understand and coordinate the needs of both offensive and defensive playstyles.

Team Strategy

Agent Composition

  • The team has a balanced distribution of agents across all roles - 2 Duelists, 1 Initiator, 1 Controller, 1 Sentinel.

  • This composition allows for strong executes onto sites with the duelists creating space, initiator gathering information, and controller locking down angles.

  • On defense, the controller and sentinel can set up utility to stall pushes while the duelists and initiator rotate to apply pressure.

Playing Style

  • With three aggressive players (ChiaWei, XyuS, Maple), the team can play a proactive style focused on taking map control early.

  • The duelists and initiator can work together to gain information and create openings for the team to capitalize on.

  • v1c and wackie provide stability on defense and can enable retakes if the aggressive players are neutralized.

Consistency and Adaptability

  • Having players from SEA, NA, and EU brings diverse perspectives and playstyles that can make the team less predictable.

  • The flexibility of ChiaWei and Maple to play multiple roles allows the team to adapt strategies based on the map or opponent.

Strength and Weakness

Strengths

  • Strength1: Aggressive playstyle and strong individual skill across all roles, especially the duelists, can overwhelm opponents.

  • Strength2: Versatility in agent composition allows for dynamic strategies and unpredictability.

  • Strength3: Regional diversity provides unique perspectives and approaches to the game.

Weaknesses

  • Weakness1: Overly aggressive playstyle could be exploited by disciplined teams that can trade effectively.

  • Weakness2: Communication challenges across different regions may impact coordination.
